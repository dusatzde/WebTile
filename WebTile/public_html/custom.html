Web Live Tile

Welcome to the Web Live Tile project website. Thanks to this project you can pin your favourite websites to your windows phone home screen. If you are unable to find your website icon in the Top section, you can make your own link in Custom section.

Supported operating systems are Windows Phone 7 and Windows Phone 8. To make custom web live tile you need to enter the URL, choose the category and pin the generated website to your screen. The service is completely free and without any ads. Nice live tiles that links to your favorite website. Enjoy!
